For five required components, define the deliberately uncomplicated score

\begin{equation}
  S = \prod_{i=1}^{5} I_i,
  \label{eq:score}
\end{equation}

where every $I_i$ is either zero or one. Equation~\ref{eq:score} becomes one
only when nothing required is missing. This fact survived internal review.
