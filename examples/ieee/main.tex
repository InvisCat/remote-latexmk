\title{Why a Successful Remote Compilation Demonstrates\\
That the Remote Compilation Succeeded}

\author{\IEEEauthorblockN{The Authors}}

\begin{abstract}
This paper studies the unsurprising hypothesis that a successful remote
compilation produces a successfully compiled document. We model success as the
conjunction of every condition required for success. The analysis finds that
when every such condition holds, the expected output is observed. The result
is intentionally modest, reproducible, and difficult to disagree with.
\end{abstract}
